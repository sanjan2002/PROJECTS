Institute of Technology and Management (Kannada: ರೇವ ತಾಂತ್ರಿಕ ಮಹಾವಿದ್ಯಾಲಯ) is a private state University located in Bangalore, Karnataka.
REVA Group of Educational Institutions was established in 2002, managed by Rukmini Educational Charitable Trust.REVA University strives for academic excellence.
Innovative pedagogy, best mentors, and faculty with vast industry experience offer modern education of global standards.
