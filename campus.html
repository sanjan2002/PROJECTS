KUVEMPU AUDITORIUM

Kuvempu Auditorium is located at CV Raman block with a seating capacity of 1500 . As the seating arrangement is at multiple levels and on raised platforms, it is easy for the spectators to view the performances on the stage. The auditorium is suitable to host performances, events, competitions, inaugural ceremonies, etc.

SAUGANDHIKA

Saugandhika is an open-air stage adorned by two gazebos on each side.The central stage is suitable for conducting huge events with lots of people as attendees. The gazebos are cylindrical and open on all sides. The gazebos are used for outdoor education so that the students can connect with nature while learning.

REVA RANGASTHALA

REVA Rangasthala is an amphitheatre with a seating capacity of 1000 .It is located at Swami Vivekananda Block with a central stage and concentric seating arrangement on raised platforms that allow the audience to have a clear view of the central stage while watching performances or events.
